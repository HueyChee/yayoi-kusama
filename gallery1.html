Infinity Mirror Rooms

Reflecting Infinite Universes

Enter Yayoi Kusama's Infinity Mirror Rooms and get lost in a mesmerizing world of infinite reflections. Mirrored walls and twinkling LED lights create an illusion of boundless space, blurring the lines between reality and imagination. Immerse yourself in this captivating sensory experience, where light and space converge to create an enchanting spectacle. Discover the magic of the infinite as you step into Kusama's captivating Infinity Mirror Rooms.
