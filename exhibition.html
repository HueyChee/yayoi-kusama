CURRENT EXHIBITIONS

Self-Obliteration / Psychedelic World

This exhibition's concept merges the self with the cosmic through repetitive patterns, vibrant colors, and mirrored surfaces, inviting viewers to experience a transformative and boundary-dissolving journey into a psychedelic realm.

Life Is the Heart of the Rainbow

This is an immersive exhibition by Yayoi Kusama that celebrates life through vibrant colors, endless patterns, and captivating installations.

One with Eternity

Yayoi Kusama's introspective exhibition delves into the concept of eternity. Kusama prompts viewers to consider the timeless nature of existence and our interconnectedness with the infinite through captivating installations and artworks.

Yayoi Kusama: 1945 to now

This exhibition's is a retrospective highlighting Japanese artist Yayoi Kusama's journey from 1945 to the present. It features her famous polka dots, immersive installations, and impact on contemporary art.
